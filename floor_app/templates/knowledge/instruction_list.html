About Instruction Rules

Instruction rules are automated business logic that evaluate conditions and trigger actions. When conditions match (e.g., "bit SMI type is X AND size is Y"), the system automatically performs actions like showing warnings, preventing operations, sending notifications, or calculating values. Rules can be default (always active) or temporary (time-limited).
