Policy Configuration Tips
  • Annual Leave (KSA): 21 days first year, 30 days after 5 years, fully paid
  • Sick Leave (KSA): 30 days 100% pay, 60 days 75% pay, 30 days unpaid
  • Maternity (KSA): 70 days (10 weeks), fully paid, min 4 weeks after delivery
  • Newborn (KSA): 3 days for father, fully paid
  • Bereavement (KSA): 5 days (spouse/parent/child), 3 days (sibling/grandparent)
  • Hajj (KSA): Once during employment, typically 2-3 weeks, unpaid unless using annual leave
