Leave Request Guidelines (KSA Labor Law)
Annual Leave:
  • Minimum 30 days advance notice
  • Maximum 21 consecutive days
  • Cannot be split into periods < 5 days (except last portion)
Sick Leave:
  • Medical certificate required for > 2 days
  • First 30 days: 100% pay
  • Next 60 days: 75% pay
  • Final 30 days: Unpaid
Maternity Leave:
  • 10 weeks (70 days) fully paid
  • Minimum 4 weeks must be after delivery
  • Medical certificate required
Emergency Leave:
  • Bereavement: 3-5 days based on relationship
  • Newborn (Father): 3 days
  • No advance notice required
