Overtime Request Guidelines
For Employees:
  • Submit requests at least 24 hours in advance when possible
  • Provide clear justification for overtime work
  • Check your quarterly limit before requesting (90 hours max per KSA law)

  • Verify work justification and business need
  • Check employee's quarterly overtime total
  • Approve only necessary overtime hours
