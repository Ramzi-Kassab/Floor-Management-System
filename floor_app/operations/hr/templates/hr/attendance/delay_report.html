Reporting Guidelines
  • Submit delay reports as soon as possible after arriving late
  • Be honest and accurate about arrival times
  • Provide specific, detailed explanations (e.g., "Heavy traffic on Highway 15 due to accident" vs just "Traffic")
  • Include supporting documentation if available (photos, receipts, etc.) - attach to email or submit separately
  • Repeated delays without valid reasons may result in disciplinary action
  • Valid emergencies are always considered (medical, family, vehicle breakdown, etc.)
